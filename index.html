FAQ

Common questions

How much does VVavy cost?

VVavy includes a free experience.

Do I need to sign up or download anything?

No download required. Open VVavy in any modern browser and start creating visuals right away, or just press play and relax.

What audio sources does VVavy support?

VVavy supports five audio inputs: uploaded audio files (MP3, WAV, FLAC, and more), SoundCloud, your device microphone for live input, system audio capture, and MIDI controllers. You can switch between sources at any time.

Can I export videos for TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts?

Yes. VVavy can export your audio and visuals as a video clip. Choose vertical 9:16 for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts or wide 16:9 for YouTube and other platforms. Perfect for music teasers, single previews, and social promos.

Can VVavy sync my webcam or live video feed to music?

Yes. Compatible visuals can blend your webcam or live video feed with beat-reactive effects like motion trails, edge treatments, scanlines, and temporal smears. It works well for streamers, dance clips, performance videos, reaction content, and stylized camera-facing posts.

Can I create my own custom visuals?

Yes. VVavy includes an AI-assisted custom visual creator, so you can bring in custom-built visuals without turning it into a whole production.

Can DJs use VVavy for live performances?

Absolutely. Connect your microphone or use system audio to capture your mix in real time. Sixty-plus visuals react instantly to every beat, drop, and frequency shift, making VVavy a natural fit for live DJ sets, club nights, and events.

Can streamers use VVavy as a stream overlay or background?

Yes. Use system audio capture to drive VVavy's visuals with your stream audio. Add VVavy as a browser source in OBS or Streamlabs as a reactive background or overlay for your Twitch, YouTube, or Kick streams, with no plugins or capture cards needed.

How can 2D/3D artists use VVavy?

Whether you work in 2D or 3D, you can bring your own visual style into VVavy and let people experience it with music in a way that feels immediate and human.

Does VVavy support Chromecast?

Yes. VVavy supports Google Cast. Cast your audio-reactive visuals to any Chromecast or Google Cast-compatible device for a full-screen experience on your TV or large display.

How many visuals does VVavy have?

VVavy has 60+ built-in audio-reactive visuals across styles including trippy WebGL tunnels, cinematic landscapes, minimal geometry, dreamy particle clouds, and euphoric neon scenes. New visuals are added regularly, and you can create your own with the AI-powered creator.

Does VVavy work on mobile?

VVavy works in any modern browser including mobile browsers. For the best experience with complex WebGL visuals, a desktop or laptop is recommended.
